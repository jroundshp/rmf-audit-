01 Company Snapshot
Founders
Jacob Shirar (Engineering, ex-Deimco Sales) + Ryan Hass (Agriculture, ex-Deimco Tech). Childhood friends from Iowa who launched the business in Craig, CO.
What They Do
Apply factory-grade stains, paints, sealers, and topcoats to building materials before job-site delivery — using a Deimco automated line for standard runs and manual spray booths for complex work.
Target Market
Colorado luxury resort corridor — Vail, Aspen, Steamboat. High-end properties that remodel every 5–10 years. Also serves contractors, distributors, lumber yards, and interior designers.
Products Finished
Siding & trim (wood, fiber cement, engineered), mouldings & millwork (MDF, Poplar, Alder, Cherry), natural wood (Cedar, Poplar), doors & jambs (fiberglass, steel, stained).
Color Offering
2,000+ solid colors + 36 RealWood faux-stain combos via PPG partnership. Custom color matching and jobsite touch-up containers available.
Positioning
"Colorado's Premier Pre-Finishing Operation." Eco-friendly water-based coatings, factory-controlled quality, 5–10 day turnaround, orders from 1 board to thousands of units.

03 Where They Rank

RMF holds a niche regional position in a market dominated by national players. Their local Colorado footprint and 5–10 day turnaround is a genuine advantage over Legacy and Metrie for Mountain West builders — but they have not yet built the brand visibility to capture that demand.

Strengths
Right geography. Deimco equipment. PPG partnership. Flexible order sizes (1 to thousands). Strong founding story and industry pedigree.
Current Position
Regional niche leader in name only — claim not yet backed by brand recognition, online visibility, or social proof.
Biggest Opportunity
No competitor owns "Mountain West pre-finishing." RMF could own that category outright with consistent SEO content and marketing.

Key Finding

The gap between their real capability and their online presence is the core problem. A contractor Googling "prefinished siding Colorado" or "pre-finished millwork Mountain West" will land on Legacy Pre-Finishing or Metrie — not RMF. They are effectively invisible to their own target market despite having the right product, equipment, and location.
